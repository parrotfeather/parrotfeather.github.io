Worried About Light Color? LEDs Cast the Same Light Color as Traditional Incandescents!

With innovations in LED technology, household LED bulbs are able to cast any color- from sunlight to traditional lamplight!


Continue

Scared That LEDs Will Take Time to Warm Up?

LEDs, unlike compact flourescents, turn on instantly, providing full light to a room without any delay!


Worried About Temperature?

LED bulbs are cool burning, meaning that even when on for extended periods of time they remain at room temperature. This is possible because of how little power they use!


Continue

Scared of Toxic Chemicals?

Unlike CFLs, the older option for efficient lighting, LEDs contain no mercury, meaning they release no toxins into the air when broken.
