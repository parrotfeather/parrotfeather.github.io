Environmental Benefits

Of LED Lighting

"Widespread use of LED lighting has the greatest potential impact on energy savings in the United States. By 2027, widespread use of LEDs could save about 348 TWh (compared to no LED use) of electricity: This is the equivalent annual electrical output of 44 large electric power plants."

- Department of Energy

How Using Inefficient Bulbs Hurts the Planet


1

Incandescent or Flourescent bulbs use up to 8 times as much power as LEDs.


2

The power is generated in power plants, which burn coal, releasing carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.


3

Carbon dioxide gathers in the atmosphere, trapping in heat in an effect known as the greenhouse effect.


4

These changed climate conditions lead to catastropic events such as hurricanes and flooding.
